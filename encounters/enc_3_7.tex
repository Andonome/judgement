\Pgls{crawler} watches the road brazenly on this clear, Sunny day.
The troupe spot it instantly, an arrow's flight away, hanging upside-down from an extremely tall tree.

\chitincrawler
\label{crawlerStatblock}

Once they move a little farther along the road, they lose sight of its tall perch.
They don't know if it still sits there, or if it's moved away from them, or if it currently stalks them.
In fact it continues sitting, motionless, like so many \glspl{crawler} do over the mild weather.
