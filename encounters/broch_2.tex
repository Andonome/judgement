\subsubsection{The Thumb \Glsfmttext{broch}}%
sticks out of the side of a hill in a way that makes everyone wonder `\textit{what was the architect thinking?}'.
The road winds up in a way that doesn't let anyone see what's below properly, so using \pgls{bow} receives +1 to the \gls{tn}.

Any complaints can be sent straight to \Gls{jotter}~\composeHumanName.
\currentName\ was born completely deaf, so all requests must be given in writing, or by sign language.
This has helped her career immensely, as she picked up the Gnollish language quickly%
\exRef{stories}{Stories}{gnollishLanguage}
(and barters with them routinely), and has a default reason to refuse both urgent and non-urgent requests.
