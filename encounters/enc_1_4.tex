\begin{boxtext}
  Thunder roars above, and strange eyes twinkle from the shadows of the forest.
  Nothing is visible except the twinkle, staring, about the height of an elf.
\end{boxtext}

Goats watch the troupe, but flee the moment anyone moves towards them or makes a loud noise.
Shooting them has \pgls{tn} of 9, and a successful shot means enough meat for 7 days.
