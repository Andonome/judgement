The air feels \emph{wrong}.
If the troupe travel with \glspl{npc}, one of them complains about having nasty headache and blames it on Gnomish \gls{alchemy}.

\begin{boxtext}
  Something in the distant darkness moves.
  Something large comes your way, racing across the open land.
\end{boxtext}

\ifodd\thefenestraDay\Pgls{basilisk}\else\Pgls{crawler}\fi\ is moving towards the troupe.
It will arrive in 3~\glspl{round}, but first, they have another problem.
The character with the longest hair notices it \emph{dancing}.

\begin{boxtext}
  Your hair stands up on the back of your neck, then the rest joins.
  Your hair waves as if underwater.
  It stands up an waves.
\end{boxtext}

The dancing hair indicates that lightning will strike soon.
Roll $1D6$ each \gls{round}.
If you roll \dicef{1}, lightning strikes the tallest person present (probably the \ifodd\thefenestraDay\gls{basilisk}\else\gls{crawler}\fi), dealing \dmg{12}~Damage.
