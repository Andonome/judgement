\chitincrawler
\label{crawlerStatblock}

\noindent
\Pgls{crawler} watches the troupe from the shadows of the forest, just 10~\glspl{step} away.
The \glspl{pc} may spot it with a \roll{Wits}{Vigilance} roll at
\setTN{Wits}{Stealth}
\tn.
\ifnum\value{r12}>\value{hp}
  The \gls{crawler} flees at the first sign of aggression.
\else
  The \gls{crawler} attacks immediately.
  Each Failure Margin on the roll removes \pgls{ap}.

  It tries to grab%
  \exRef{core}{Core Rules}{grab}
  \pgls{pc}, flee, and entangle them in a web.
  It eats the \gls{pc} in $1D6$ days.
\fi
